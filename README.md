# Vedant-VIVA-California-Ecommerce-Dashboard
Visualized meaningful insights for Viva California, regarding sales data. Details of Product sales, Order mode, Order delivery, Region wise sales, etc has been visualized.

**Insights and KPI's
1. Total Orders
2. Total Quantity
3. Total Amount
4. Avg. Rating
5. Avg. Days to Deliver

**Questions

2. How our customers like to buy
3. How many they buy?
4. Which products are popular?
5. Overall gender split
6. Where do our customers live?
7. How long we take to ship the orders
8. How happy are our customers?
